UX Research at Viacom18 – Voot

Summary: Led UX research for Voot’s mobile experience. Used eye tracking, analytics (Mixpanel), and qualitative interviews to redesign content layout, improve signup, and reduce navigation friction. Conducted segmentation studies based on platform and user demographics to inform design strategies.

Aim: Optimize content discovery and increase signup rates across Android and iOS apps.

Methods: Eye tracking (Tobii), Mixpanel funnel tracking, A/B testing, interviews.

Results: Achieved measurable gains in content exploration depth and a significant increase in successful signups.
