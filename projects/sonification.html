Sonification of Learning

Summary: Research project exploring auditory feedback to support cognitive tasks in visual-spatial learning. We developed and tested a sonification-enhanced auditory system and assessed its impact on mental load, perceived learning, and engagement in a learning interface, using both subjective surveys and performance metrics.

Aim: Enhance learning interfaces by integrating non-intrusive, real-time audio feedback.

Methods: Experimental design comparing baseline vs sonified task performance, using NASA-TLX and custom perception surveys.

Results: Sonified feedback improved engagement and subjective understanding while maintaining performance.
